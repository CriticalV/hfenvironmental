Do You Currently Own A Septic Tank?

Septic tank owners in England need to check if their tanks comply with recent changes in law governing off-mains drainage. If they don’t, they may find themselves open to prosecution and/or problems with the sale or rental of their property.

From January 2020, it became illegal for any tank in England to discharge effluent directly into a watercourse such as a river, stream or pond; any that do must be replaced or upgraded.

The changes were not widely publicised and it appears that many owners are still not clear on the full ramifications. For further information please visit the government website.
